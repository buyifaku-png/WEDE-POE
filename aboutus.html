Our Story

Providing Quality Tech Solutions Since 2021.

Who We Are

Founded in 2021 in the heart of Gqeberha, Mzansi Tech Repairs began with a simple mission: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable electronic repair services to the local community. We recognized that in a digital world, a broken device isn't just an inconvenience—it's a barrier to education and work.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality technical repairs with transparency and integrity, ensuring that every customer in Gqeberha stays connected.

Our Vision

To become the leading tech service provider in the Eastern Cape, known for our technical expertise and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Why Mzansi Tech?

Whether you are a student at Nelson Mandela University needing a laptop fix or a business professional with a cracked smartphone screen, our certified technicians treat every repair with the same level of precision and care. We use only premium parts and offer a guarantee on all our work.
